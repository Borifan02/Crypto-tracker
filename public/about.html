About Crypto Tracker

Crypto Tracker simplifies access to cryptocurrency market data. Our mission is to present complex market information clearly.

Purpose

  • Track live cryptocurrency prices and trends.
  • Display historical charts showing coin performance.
  • Enable easy comparisons across multiple coins.

Tech Stack

Frontend: Vanilla JS + Chart.js. Backend: Node.js/Express proxy. Optional storage: SQLite / MongoDB.
